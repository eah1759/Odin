Not Your Boyfriend's Pasta

Description

Pasta is pasta. The name is an inside joke. I am not implying that this pasta is alcoholic in any way. It's quite a soupy pasta though.

Credit where credit is due, this recipe is taken from a bee.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Chopped Celery
  • Chopped Onions
  • Chopped/Sliced Carrots
  • 0.5-1.5 Pounds Ground Beef
  • 60oz Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Pound Linguine/Fettucini Pasta
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix the chopped vegetables in a pot.
  2. Cover the mixture in olive oil (Enough that all the vegetables have oil over them but not enough that there is a pool of oil).
  3. Fry the mixture on medium high heat until soft and brown.
  4. Put in the ground beef and some salt into the mixture (Make sure to break up the ground beef).
  5. While the meat cooks, boil a pot of water.
  6. When the water is boiling, add the pasta into it to cook.
  7. Once the meat is browned, put all the tomato sauce into the mixture.
  8. When the pasta is done, drain it and add it to the sauce.
