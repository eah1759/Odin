チキンカツ「Chicken Katsu」

Description

Chicken Katsu is a Japanese fried chicken cutlet dish that goes well with miso soup. It's surprisingly easy to make, requiring at most half an hour of prep and cook time, and only requiring a stove-top and pan (and the ingredients, of course).

Credit where credit is due, this recipe is taken from sakuraiiko.

Ingredients

  • Skinless, Boneless Chicken Breasts (halves)
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Panko (bread crumbs)
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts with the salt and pepper.
  2. Dredge the chicken breasts in flour.
  3. Coat the chicken breasts in egg.
  4. Press the chicken breasts into the Panko.
  5. Fry the chicken breasts in the cooking oil until gliden brown.
