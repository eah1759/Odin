味噌汁「Miso Soup」

Description

Miso Soup is a Japanese soup. Love some good stove top soup. Mmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmm soup.

Credit where credit is due, this vague recipe is taken from "Allrecipes Member".

Ingredients

  • Dashi Granules
  • Miso Paste (Typically Red)
  • Tofu
  • Green Onions
  • Water

Steps

  1. Chop the tofu into medium sized cubes.
  2. Chop the green onions.
  3. Mix four cups of water with the dashi granules in a pot.
  4. Heat the mixture to just below a boil.
  5. Whisk the miso paste into the mixture.
  6. Add the tofu and green onions into the mixture.
  7. Simmer until ready.
